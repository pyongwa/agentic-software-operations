## Rules
- **One comment / one message per period** — one row of history per reporting
  unit; never merge periods.
- **Verified vs. inferred** — mark every claim. A status taken from the tracker is
  *verified*; a status reconstructed from indirect signals is *inferred* and
  labeled so. Reconstructed history is never presented as verified fact.
- **Draft-first** — produce the full report and show it; post nothing without
  explicit approval.
